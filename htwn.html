Popular Attractions

Marina Beach

The only drive in beach Kerala. Considered as a swimmers paradise, this is the perfect beach to relax and be soaked in bliss. The beach is well maintained and clean.

Jagannath Temple

A small trail on the side of the railway track leads to the temple. Jagannath Temple was opened to Harijans in the 1920s, while many temples in the country still deny entry to Scheduled castes and tribes.
